# Product Story: Media Ops Mission Control

## Core Promise

Paramount Media Ops Mission Control is a control plane for streaming incident intelligence and RCA-driven actioning.

It helps operators do one job end-to-end:

1. Detect the highest-impact incident.
2. Explain root cause with normalized evidence.
3. Recommend the next best action with confidence and business impact.
4. Route risky actions through explicit approval workflow.
5. Generate and execute verification tests.
6. Track recovery outcomes with full auditability.

## Why This Product Exists

Traditional operations dashboards show many metrics, but they do not close the loop from signal to verified recovery.  
This platform turns incident response into a measurable decision loop:

- **Signal -> Evidence -> Decision -> Governance -> Execution -> Outcome**

## Operator Outcomes

- Faster triage with prioritized incident queue.
- Lower error rates through risk-aware action approvals.
- Reduced repeat incidents via targeted regression generation.
- Governance-ready audit trail for architecture and compliance reviews.

## Product Focus

The platform is intentionally centered on streaming incident intelligence and RCA operations.

- Mission Control and incident detail views are primary.
- Secondary AI capabilities live in dedicated feature areas and docs.
- Deterministic scoring rules are default for critical control-plane decisions.

## Who This Is For

- SRE and platform operations leads
- Incident commanders
- QA and release engineering
- Engineering management reviewing cycle-time and recovery performance
